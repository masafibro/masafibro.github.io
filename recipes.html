Chocolate chip cookies

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups or 455 grams sofetend, unsalted butter
  • 2 cups white sugar
  • 2 cups brown sugar
  • 4 large eggs
  • 4 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 2 teaspoons baking soda
  • 4 teaspoons hot water
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 6 cups all purpose flour
  • 2 cups semi-sweet chocolate chips
  • 2 cups milk chocolate chips

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 170 degrees celcius
  2. cream butter and sugar until smooth and fluffy
  3. beat eggs in one at a time
  4. stir in vanilla
  5. dissolve baking soda in water and add to cookie dough
  6. add in salt, and flour and mix
  7. add chocolate chips
  8. shape dough into golf ball sized spheres and place on baking paper
  9. place dough 1 inch apart on baking sheet and bake for 9-10 minutes
  10. let cool for 10 minutes on a wire rack
  11. Enjoy! :)
